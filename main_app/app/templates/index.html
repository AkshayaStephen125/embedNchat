Enterprise-Ready Multi-Tenant AI Chatbot Platform


TenantAI is a secure, scalable AI chatbot microservice built for modern enterprises. It enables multiple organizations to integrate AI-powered chat experiences into their applications while maintaining strict data isolation and tenant-level security.

Designed using FastAPI and modern AI architectures, TenantAI supports document-based intelligence, API-first integrations, and seamless onboarding for companies of any size. Each tenant operates in a fully isolated environment, ensuring privacy, compliance, and performance.

Whether you are building internal knowledge bots, customer support assistants, or AI copilots, TenantAI gives you a production-ready foundation without compromising control or scalability.

How It Works

1. Tenant Onboarding

Organizations sign up and securely create their tenant workspace. Each tenant receives isolated storage, AI indexes, and API access.

2. Upload & Index Documents

Upload PDFs or documents which are processed, converted into embeddings, and stored in a tenant-specific vector database.

3. Chat & Integrate

Ask questions via UI or API. Queries are matched against tenant data, and AI responses are generated securely using LLMs.
