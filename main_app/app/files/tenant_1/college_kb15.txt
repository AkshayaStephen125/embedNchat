Greenfield Institute Knowledge Base

Institution Overview:
Greenfield Institute of Technology & Management is located in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala.
Established in 2002, it provides undergraduate and postgraduate education with strong focus on placements, practical learning, and industry readiness.
